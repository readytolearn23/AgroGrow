What is AgroGrow ?

In India, agriculture is the main source of economy and employment. India is the 2nd largest agricultural producer and 7th largest exporter of agricultural goods. This project aims to help the farmers to cultivate the best type of crop based on physical external conditions of the soil and environment for a better and healthy crop yield production.

The major purpose of our project is to create a user-friendly website.
The user may take a soil sample to a local agriculture lab to have their soil health evaluated.
In the case of sickness, the photographs of the leaf will suffice as input.
Indian farmers do not opt for the crops according to the soil necessities.
Using a Crop Selection Method (CSM) solves the crop selection problem and improves the net yield rate of the crop
